You chose to explore the druid's glen

You choose to explore. You foot step onto the mossy path, your boots sink slightly. Haggis accompanies you. You walk around and find an old carving in a tree, of a woman in chains, face unfinished, beside a monster. Darkness falls into your eyes. As you sit beneath the tree, your hand brushes something cold beneath the leaves. You dig gently. A **compass**. Its surface is scratched, its dial strange it does not point north. It points to something else. **Truth, perhaps. Or fate.** You slip it into your pocket, you keep sailing, with resources and motive
