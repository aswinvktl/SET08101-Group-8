⚠️ Before You Begin

Loch Ness Monster: Expedition is a narrative journey, it's a completely fictional story set in a fantasy world. It draws from Scottish folklore and landscapes, reimagined through personal experience and emotion, with fantasy elements.

This story explores mature and sometimes intense themes including:

  • Loneliness and loss
  • Grief and memory
  • Transformation and identity
  • Magic, war, and recovery
  • Mentions of death, drowning, and mythical creatures (including scenes of being hunted or consumed by monsters)

To understand the terminology and creative choices, please visit the FAQ page before starting.

Otherwise, continue when you are ready
